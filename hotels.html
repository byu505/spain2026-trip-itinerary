Sevilla Mon 5/18 – Thu 5/21

Only areas with 4.5+ star hotels. Stay central to walk to Barrio Santa Cruz, Cathedral, Alcázar, and the river.

Central / Santa Cruz

Rating: ★★★★½ (4.5/5) — hotels in this area

Proximity: 2–5 min walk to Cathedral, Alcázar, Barrio Santa Cruz; 10 min to Plaza de España; 15 min to Triana. Best for fitting the full Sevilla itinerary on foot.

Narrow streets, tapas nearby. Look for options near Plaza de Santa Cruz, Calle Sierpes, or just east of the Cathedral. Book early for May.

Typical price: €90–180/night mid-range; €200+ upscale.

Search: “Santa Cruz Sevilla” or “centro histórico Sevilla” on your preferred booking site.

Madrid Thu 5/21 – Mon 5/25

Only areas with 4.5+ star hotels. Stay near the center for Prado, Retiro, Royal Palace, and La Latina.

La Latina / Sol

Rating: ★★★★½ (4.5/5) — hotels in this area

Proximity: 5–10 min to Mercado de San Miguel and Cava Baja bars; 15 min to Prado and Royal Palace; 5 min to Sol/Gran Vía. Best for tapas, nightlife, and central sights.

Very central and lively in the evening. Fits the La Latina and San Miguel itinerary and nightlife.

Typical price: €100–200/night mid-range; €220+ upscale.

Search: “La Latina Madrid” or “Sol Madrid hotel”.

Huertas / Paseo del Prado

Rating: ★★★★½ (4.5/5) — hotels in this area

Proximity: 2–5 min to Prado Museum; 10 min to Retiro Park; 15 min to La Latina and Sol. Ideal for museum and park days.

Slightly quieter, good for museum days and a calm base. Still walkable to bars and clubs.

Typical price: €110–220/night mid-range.

Search: “Huertas Madrid” or “Paseo del Prado hotel”.

Booking tips

  • Prices are approximate for late May; book sooner for better selection and rates.
  • Compare Booking.com, Hotels.com, and direct hotel sites; sometimes direct is cheaper.
  • If you’re sharing rooms (e.g. Brandon & DJ + Bridgette in Sevilla), filter for 2+ guests and check cancellation policies.
  • Check whether breakfast is included; in both cities you may prefer going out for coffee and pastries.
